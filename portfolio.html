I created this website from scratch using HTML and CSS to style the pages. I followed a YouTube tutorial, from which I tweaked and added my personal touch. The site is being hosted on Github.

The first time I dabbled with this program was as a graphic design team member at Watonomous - an autonomous vehicle student design team! I also practiced with it while redesigning a logo for eMentorship, a subdivision of the Accounting and Financial Management Student Association - Internal Services where I was head of Marketing. I used Illustrator to design the graphics on this site, including the bao home logo in the navigation pane, the cartoon of myself for the About section, and the resume icon on the homepage. I have also drew the TaxPrep logos seen in the "Accounting student" section below.

I wanted this website to be clean, and minimalistic but still have some personality. Initially, I thought that the typewriter style font would be fitting of the minimalist theme however I did debate whether a sleek, modern font would fit the look I am trying to achieve better.
